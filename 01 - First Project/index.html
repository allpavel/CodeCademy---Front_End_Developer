Jaguar's first all-electric performance SUV

The I-PACE is a 5-seater sports car. One full charge provides a range of up to 480km. Instant torque and all-wheel drive traction enable it to accelerate up to 100km/h in 4.8 seconds just like a sports car. It’s propelled by two permanent magnet synchronous electric motors, similar to a Formula E.

"Every Jaguar feels like no other car on the road."
I-PACE reimagines the classic Jaguar balance of sports car focus and luxury craftsmanship for the digital age.

Throughout the interior, beautiful, handcrafted touches reaffirm the cabin’s luxury atmosphere. Rich, solid finishers surround you and fine contrast twin-needle stitching adds tailored sophistication. At night, crisp white illumination accentuates the exquisite details.
